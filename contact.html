FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enrol in a course?

You can enrol directly from any course page. Once enrolled, you'll receive immediate access to all course materials via our online learning platform.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes — our platform is fully mobile-optimised. You can learn from any device, wherever you are.

Do courses offer CME credits?

Many of our programs are CME-eligible. Each course page clearly indicates whether credits are available and the number of credits offered.

What's included in the membership?

Membership includes full course library access, live webinars, clinical tools, community access, and event discounts. See our Membership page for full details.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period with no cancellation fees.

Do you offer team or group pricing?

Yes — our Elite membership includes up to 3 seats. For larger teams, contact us directly for custom group pricing.
